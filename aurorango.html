What is AURORA?

AURORA is an NGO based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, dedicated to empowering women through education, skill development, and legal aid. We believe in uplifting women by providing opportunities for growth and independence.

Our Vision

To create a society where every woman has equal rights, opportunities, and freedom to make her own choices, fostering a world of dignity, equality, and empowerment.

Our Mission

To uplift women by providing education, vocational training, and legal awareness, ensuring they lead independent and empowered lives.

About the Founder

HARISH is the sole founder of AURORA, driven by a passion for women's empowerment. With a vision to create a better world for women, Harish established AURORA to provide education, skill development, and legal aid.
